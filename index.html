Studio Features

  • 1. BYOD (Bring Your Own Device): Flexibly connect your personal laptop for a customized setup while leveraging professional-grade equipment.
  • 2. Podium PC: Pre-configured for seamless use with popular teaching and presentation software, ensuring an easy start for every session.
  • 3. Big Monitor: 38” curved display provides ample screen real estate for multitasking, managing presentations, or viewing detailed content with ease.
  • 4. Quiet Space: Soundproofed environment minimizes distractions, allowing for clear communication and professional audio quality.
  • 5. Flexible Setup: Adjustable components like lights, microphones, and monitors enable a tailored experience suited to your unique presentation style.
  • 6. Customizable Controls: The StreamDeck provides quick access to personalized settings, streamlining your workflow during presentations or recordings.
